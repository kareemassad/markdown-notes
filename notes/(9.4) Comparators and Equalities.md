# (9.4) Comparators and Equalities

```js
=== //is equal to 
!== //is not equal to
>   //greater than
<   //Less than

>=  //Greater or equal to
<=  //lesser or equal to
```
###### NOTE:
== checks for equality
=== checks for equality and same data types

### Combining Comparators

```js
&&  //AND
||  //OR
!   //NOT
```
